Vision & Mission

oCAD's Vision:
To democratize 3D design through AI, making professional-grade 3D asset creation accessible to everyone.

oCAD's Mission:
Empowering businesses to transform ideas into 3D reality instantly through AI.

Our text2CAD technology represents a paradigm shift in how businesses approach 3D design and prototyping. By leveraging advanced AI models, we've created a system that understands design intent, engineering constraints, and manufacturing requirements.

The market opportunity is clear: businesses across industries need faster ways to create and iterate on 3D assets. Traditional 3D modeling is time-consuming and requires specialized skills. Our AI solution breaks these barriers, enabling rapid prototyping and design iteration.

Our technology excels in two key areas:

  1. 1. Revolutionary 3D Asset Generation
    Our text2CAD AI transforms written descriptions into detailed 3D models, complete with proper dimensions, materials, and manufacturing considerations. This dramatically accelerates the prototyping process and reduces design iteration cycles.
    The system learns from each interaction, continuously improving its understanding of design requirements and industry standards.

  2. 2. Intelligent Design Optimization
    Beyond basic generation, our AI optimizes designs for manufacturability, structural integrity, and cost-effectiveness. It suggests improvements and alternatives based on industry best practices.
    This ensures that generated models aren't just visually accurate but also practical for real-world applications.

We believe these aspects will fundamentally alter industries through AI, and such changes are essential for technology to evolve into a new generation of platforms—"from task-specific AI to general AI." AI will lead us into a world of limitless possibilities, and this transformation is already underway...
